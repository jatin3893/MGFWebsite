When is gold an appropriate gift?

Gifting precious metals like Gold & Silver coins is considered as a token of love, trust and appreciation. Traditionally, Gold Coins are given as wedding gifts, and also are given to newborns.

There is no better start for a couple just getting married, than to receive some gold as a savings for future use.

Graduations are also an appropriate time, as that's when a young person starts out in the world, and shouldn't have to do so empty-handed.
