What is a travel card?
Travel cards are a popular, convenient and secure way to buy foreign currency and take it overseas. Essentially, travel money cards are specially designed debit cards that you load up with foreign currencies prior to travelling.
Why use a prepaid travel card?

It’s safer. A prepaid card is similar to having cash on you but it’s safer than carrying around too much or leaving it in your hotel even if it’s in the room safe.

Once abroad, you can reload your card or change the currencies around via the internet or your mobile phone. You can use your card to withdraw cash from ATMs, shop for souvenirs, pay for restaurant meals or book accommodation online.
